PRIORITASK

PrioriTask is an application program for computers that allows users to manage their tasks. Its unique feature is its ability to automatically update priorities of tasks based on how close they are to the given deadline. This application was built for a software engineering module called CS2103. The requirements of the module required that we build upon an existing application called Address Book that had around 6000 lines of code. I completed the project in a team of 4 and implemented the major feature of recurring tasks. Click here to find out more information on the project.

MIND YOUR LANGUAGE

Mind Your Language is a Telegram bot that reminds users of a group chat to mind their language. To elaborate further, if a person in the group chat uses a curse word, the bot will send a message to remind him or her not to swear. The bot keeps track of how many times a person swears, and it has the ability to kick a person out of the group if they swear enough times. This bot was built in a team of 4 during a two-day hackathon. Click here to find out more information on the project.
